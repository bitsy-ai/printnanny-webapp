Floating labels

Wrap a pair of <input class="form-control"> and <label> elements in .form-floating to enable floating labels with Bootstrapâ€™s textual form fields. A placeholder is required on each <input> as our method of CSS-only floating labels uses the :placeholder-shown pseudo-element. Also note that the <input> must come first so we can utilize a sibling selector (e.g., ~).

Inline Form

Use the .row-cols-lg-auto, .g-3 & .align-items-center class to display a series of labels, form controls, and buttons on a single horizontal row. Form controls within inline forms vary slightly from their default states. Controls only appear inline in viewports that are at least 576px wide to account for narrow viewports on mobile devices.

Horizontal form label sizing

Be sure to use .col-form-label-sm or .col-form-label-lg to your <label>s or <legend>s to correctly follow the size of .form-control-lg and .form-control-sm.
